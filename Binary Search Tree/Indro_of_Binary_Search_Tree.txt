#BINARY SEARCH TREE
Def: In BST, for every node, key in left side are smaller and key in right side are greater
> In BST, Data is organised in such a way so that when you are searching an item, we can skip half of the items.
> All keys are typically consider as distinct
> like linked list, it is a linked data structure
            50
        30      70
    10     40 60    80

NOTE: Most of the operation in BST such as insert,delete and searching and finding closest of all in O(h), where h=> hight of BST
NOTE2: Height of BST matters a lot
